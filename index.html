ENVOYAGE - IGNITING INGENUITY

LEAD presents Envoyage, an extraordinary two-day extravaganza that promises to captivate the new batch by chalking out an unforgettable experience as participants embark on a journey to discover the secrets of leadership during our exclusive "Get To Know " session. This will comprise an informative and beneficial talk session by the visionary Founder and Director of DRONACHARYA IAS, Mr. Anurag Bachan and will be followed by a team-based event wherein the participants will have the chance to immerse themselves in the scintillating world of auctions, as well as the chance to bid on extraordinary entrepreneurs and present their ideas through elevator pitches.

About The Speaker

LEAD presents an incredible opportunity to be inspired and guided by the esteemed Mr. AnuragBachan, the visionary Founder and Director of DRONACHARYA IAS CHANDIGARH. Prepare to be enlightened as he imparts his invaluable wisdom to students, igniting their path to success. For the past 15+ years, his DRONACHARYA-IAS has provided quality education to civil service applicants and has played a significant role in instilling in the students the knowledge base, abilities, and attitudes required to serve the nation as civil servants. This extraordinary session is poised to unlock their true potential, empowering them to reach new heights of excellence with unparalleled clarity and profound insight.

Rulebook

1. Venture Wise, an offline Auction based event, will be conducted on 3rd September 2023 from 3:00 PM onwards at TAN Audi.
2. Participants participating in teams must form teams of size of 3-4 members.
3. The teams involved will be assigned specific domains through a random allocation process.
4. Teams will be granted the privilege of exclusively selecting products and technologies that pertain to their specific domain by bidding.
5. The participating teams will have a unique opportunity to bid for entrepreneurs with the knowledge to improve their product's growth trajectory significantly.
6. The participating teams must navigate through checkpoints to meet the criteria for advancing to subsequent rounds.
7. Following the conclusion of the preliminary rounds, teams will pool their available resources and present their company to the esteemed panel of judges by preparing an elevator pitch.
8. Arguing, insulting, or harassing organizers may result in disqualification by the judges. Participating teams are kindly requested to follow timely instructions.
9. The selection committee shall determine the victor by evaluating their arguments' soundness, communication adeptness, and the idea's caliber.
10. If there is a stalemate between rounds, Quizzical Quest will assist teams in breaking the tie and advancing in the game.
11. All participants are requested to join the Discord server of envoyage for updates.
